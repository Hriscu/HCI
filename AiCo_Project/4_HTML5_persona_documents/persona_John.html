Persona - John, Airsoft Referee

Profile

  • Name: John
  • Age: 30
  • Gender: Male
  • Profession: Physical Education Teacher
  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Physical Education
  • Interests: Airsoft, technology, sharing knowledge with others

Background

John has been playing airsoft for several years and is passionate about the sport. He is interested in sharing his knowledge with others and using technology to improve the airsoft experience. Recently, he has taken up the role of a referee for airsoft matches and is responsible for ensuring that the rules are followed and that the game is played in a fair manner.

Goals

  • Use the airsoft mobile application to manage airsoft matches.
  • Set up match rules and communicate with players.
  • Track the progress of matches and keep score.
  • Provide feedback to players to help them improve their skills.

Frustrations

  • Cannot communicate effectively with players during matches.
  • Difficult to keep track of the progress of matches and keep score.
  • Cannot provide effective feedback to players after matches.

Scenario

  1. John is refereeing an airsoft match and wants to use the airsoft mobile application to manage the match.
  2. He opens the app and navigates to the "Arenas" section.
  3. He selects the ongoing match and sees the list of players who are playing.
  4. He then navigates to the "Matches" section and sets up the match rules, such as the game type, match duration, and score limit.
  5. He communicates the rules to the players using the app's messaging feature.
  6. During the match, he uses the app to keep track of the progress of the match and keep score.
  7. He also provides feedback to players using the app's feedback feature after the match is over.

Use Case

John uses the airsoft mobile application to manage airsoft matches, set up match rules, communicate with players, track the progress of matches, keep score, and provide feedback to players to help them improve their skills.
