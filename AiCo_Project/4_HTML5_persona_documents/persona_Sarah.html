Persona - Sarah, Airsoft Enthusiast

Profile

  • Name: Sarah
  • Age: 21
  • Gender Female
  • Profession: College Student
  • Education: Pursuing Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design
  • Interests: Airsoft, social media, exploring new arenas

Background

Sarah has been playing airsoft for three years and considers herself an avid enthusiast. She first discovered airsoft through a friend who invited her to play in a local arena. Since then, she has been exploring new arenas and game types to challenge herself and improve her skills. In addition to playing airsoft, Sarah enjoys documenting her experiences on social media and connecting with other enthusiasts. She hopes to continue growing her skills and network in the airsoft community as she pursues her degree in Graphic Design.

Goals

  • Find new arenas to play in.
  • Explore different game types.
  • Connect with other airsoft enthusiasts.
  • Track personal performance and improve skills.
  • Share airsoft experiences on social media.

Frustrations

  • Difficulty finding new arenas to play in.
  • Lack of tools to track personal performance and learn from mistakes.
  • Difficulty connecting with other airsoft enthusiasts.

Scenario

  1. Sarah wants to explore a new airsoft arena and find players to team up with.
  2. She opens the airsoft mobile application and navigates to the "Arenas" section.
  3. She filters the list of arenas by location and chooses a nearby one that she hasn't visited before.
  4. She then navigates to the "Game Types" section and chooses "Domination" as the game type.
  5. She sees a list of players who are interested in playing "Domination" in that arena and sends them a message to team up.
  6. She also navigates to the "Performance" section and checks her personal performance statistics from her previous games.
  7. She uses the statistics to identify areas of improvement and sets goals for her next game.
  8. Finally, she takes a photo of her team after the game and shares it on her social media accounts using the airsoft mobile application's sharing feature.

Use Case

  • Find new arenas to play in using the "Arenas" section.
  • Explore different game types using the "Game Types" section.
  • Connect with other airsoft enthusiasts by sending messages to interested players.
  • Track personal performance and improve skills using the "Performance" section.
  • Share airsoft experiences on social media using the sharing feature.
