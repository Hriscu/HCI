Persona - Mark, Airsoft Arena Owner

Profile

  • Name: Mark
  • Age: 45
  • Gender: Male
  • Profession: Business Owner
  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Business Administration
  • Interests: Airsoft, business strategy, marketing

Background

Mark is the owner of an airsoft arena and has been in the business for several years. He is passionate about airsoft and is interested in providing the best possible experience to his customers. He is responsible for managing the arena, scheduling matches, and coordinating with players.

Goals

  • Use the airsoft mobile application to manage the arena.
  • Schedule matches using the app.
  • Communicate with players using the app.
  • Track the progress of matches using the app.
  • Keep score using the app.
  • Collect feedback from players using the app to improve the business.

Frustrations

  • Cannot communicate effectively with players.
  • Scheduling conflicts.
  • Difficulty keeping track of match progress and score.
  • Cannot collect feedback from players.
  • Unable to improve the business.

Scenario

  1. Mark opens the airsoft mobile application.
  2. Mark navigates to the "Arenas" section.
  3. Mark selects his arena from the list of available arenas.
  4. Mark navigates to the "Matches" section.
  5. Mark sees the list of upcoming matches for his arena.
  6. Mark schedules a new match, specifying the game type, match duration, and other details.
  7. Mark communicates the match details to the players using the app's messaging feature.
  8. Mark uses the app to keep track of the progress of the match and keep score.
  9. After the match is over, Mark collects feedback from the players using the app's feedback feature.
  10. Mark uses the feedback to improve his business.

Use Case

Mark uses the airsoft mobile application to manage his airsoft arena, schedule matches, communicate with players, track the progress of matches, keep score, and collect feedback from players to improve his business.
